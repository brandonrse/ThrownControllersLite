What is Thrown Controllers?


Thrown Controllers is a fun video game trivia game show hosted by The Runaway Guys.


The goal is to get 10 questions correct without getting a single one wrong. Do that, and you are the winner.




Thrown Controllers Lite


This is an unofficial recreation of the game show. Made to practice some coding. There are differences between this version and Proton Jon's: right now, there is only Easy Questions, Tricky Questions, Brutal Questions, Lightning Round, and Multiple Choice implemented.

The questions are also largely Nintendo-oriented questions due to my lack of knowledge on most non-Nintendo games. As a result, feel free to submit questions. If desired, you will be credited.



I will say a large reason why I made this, in addition to greatly enjoying watching Thrown Controllers, was because of the fact that Proton Jon will never be able to participate in his own game show.



Contact me on discord (SecretAdept#0699) to make suggestions and/or report any bugs, whether it be typos, grammar issues, formatting errors, etc.
